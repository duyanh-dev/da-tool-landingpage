The Story

From Tab Chaos To A Personal Desktop Laboratory

As a developer, I used to open dozens of browser tabs every day: CodePen for UI testing, format converter websites for JSON and SCSS, and Notion for task management. This fragmented workflow constantly broke my deep-work state.

Instead of relying on disconnected external tools, I decided to build my own desktop laboratory - one place that brings together everything I need for daily frontend work.
